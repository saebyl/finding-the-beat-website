Codexi is built for your spiritual practice — a space that's yours. We collect only what we need to make the app work, we never sell your data, and your practice content belongs to you. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and your rights over it.

01

Who We Are

Codexi is operated by Finding The Beat LLC, a sole-proprietor software company. "We," "us," and "our" refer to Finding The Beat LLC. "You" refers to any person who creates an account or uses the Codexi application.

Questions about this policy can be directed to us at the contact address at the bottom of this page.

02

03

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and operate the app — authenticating your account, storing your practice content, syncing across devices.
  • Improve the product — understanding which features are used and where friction exists, so we can make Codexi better.
  • Diagnose and fix errors — crash reports and error logs (via Sentry) help us identify and resolve bugs quickly.
  • Communicate with you — account-related emails only (verification, password reset, important policy changes). We will not send marketing email without your explicit opt-in.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service — detecting abuse or policy violations.
  • Legal compliance — meeting applicable legal obligations if required.

We do not use your data to train AI models, sell advertising, or share it with data brokers.

04

Third-Party Services

We use the following service providers to operate Codexi. Each has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

  • Supabase — database and authentication infrastructure. Your account credentials and practice content are stored on Supabase-managed servers.
    supabase.com/privacy
  • Sentry — error and crash monitoring. Sentry receives device and error context when the app encounters a problem.
    sentry.io/privacy
  • Resend — transactional email delivery (account verification, password resets). Your email address is shared with Resend solely to deliver these messages.
    resend.com/privacy
  • Analytics provider (future) — we plan to add an analytics tool to understand app usage at an aggregate level. We will update this policy and notify you before doing so.

We do not share your personally identifiable information with any third party for their own marketing or advertising purposes.

05

Your Practice Content

The rituals, journals, workings, and all other content you create in Codexi are yours. We do not claim ownership of your content, and we do not read it for commercial purposes.

Content you create is stored as private by default and is only accessible to your account unless you explicitly choose to share it. Seed content provided by the Codexi Library is read-only reference material we provide for your practice.

You may export or delete your content at any time — see Your Rights below.

06

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on servers managed by Supabase. We implement Row Level Security (RLS) policies on our database so that your data is only accessible to your own account — queries from other users cannot return your content.

Authentication tokens are stored in encrypted secure storage on your device. Passwords are hashed; we never store them in plaintext.

No method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We will notify you of any breach affecting your personal data as required by applicable law.

07

Data Retention

We retain your account and practice content for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information and practice content within 30 days, except where legally required to retain it.

Crash and error reports in Sentry are retained according to Sentry's standard policies (typically 90 days). Aggregate, anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely.

08

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request that we delete your account and associated personal data.
  • Portability — request your practice content in a portable format.
  • Objection — object to certain types of processing, including analytics.
  • Withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days and will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

09

Children's Privacy

Codexi is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has created an account without parental consent, please contact us and we will delete the account promptly.

10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes — such as adding a new analytics provider or changing how we handle your practice content — we will notify you via email and/or an in-app notice at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

Continued use of Codexi after the effective date of a revised policy constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.
