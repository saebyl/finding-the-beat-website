Friends & Family Beta — April 2026

Codexi is a spiritual practice management app and digital grimoire — a single place to organize your components, workings, rituals, and journals, with a curated reference library built in from the start.

Whether you're tracking a blend you've been perfecting, documenting a ritual, or keeping a reflective journal, Codexi keeps everything connected and yours.

Right now, a small group of friends and community members are testing the app ahead of a wider beta in September. Thank you for being part of it.
